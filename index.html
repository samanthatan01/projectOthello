How to Play OTHELLO

  • Each player has a color, and the aim of the game is to get more of your pieces on the board than the opponent's pieces.
  • Number of players required: 2
  • At the start of the game, 2 black pieces and 2 white pieces will be placed in the middle of the 8x8 board.
  • The player 1 (black) will make the first move to start capturing player 2’s (white) piece by surrounding the white piece(s) with a black piece either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
  • If the move is valid, player 2’s white piece(s) will be captured i.e flipped over to black, increasing the number of black pieces on the number.
  • The game ends when: The board is full/ Both players say ‘pass’/ One player gives up
  • The winner is determined by the most # of pieces on the board when the game ends
